GreenLoop.

NOSMO GreenLoop turns selected automotive plastic waste into reusable manufacturing material. The first target is practical ecology: clean feedstock, traceable processing, pellet or filament output, real test prints and visible prototypes.

GreenLoop route

The page presents GreenLoop as a developing ecological technology route, not a finished factory. The direction is clear: recycling → material → filament/pellet → reinforced printing → prototypes → future manufacturing.
