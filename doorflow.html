Why this matters

Doors are small items that create large coordination problems.

Each door can carry location, fire rating, hardware, certification, snag evidence, photo proof and final sign-off. DoorFlow turns that into a visible workflow instead of another spreadsheet chase.

01

Plan matching

Match door references from drawings with the door schedule and floor context.

02

Status layer

Show what is not started, in progress, complete, blocked or requiring evidence.

03

Evidence loop

Attach photos, notes, snags and sign-off actions to the correct door ID.

Workflow

Plan → Schedule → Match → Install → Evidence → Sign-off

Input

Upload PDF plans, Excel schedules and site photos.

Map

NEXUS links references, floor levels, rooms, ratings and required hardware.

Execute

Site team updates installation status, issues and missing items.

Verify

Final evidence and sign-off sit against the door, not in a lost message thread.

Vision

DoorFlow is not just doors. It is the construction execution pattern.

The same structure can extend into other trades: fire stopping, M&E assets, joinery packages, snagging, inspections and handover evidence.
