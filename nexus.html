Future integration direction

FabStation — industrial AR and fabrication-floor intelligence

FabStation describes its platform as augmented reality software for steel fabrication, using 3D model visualisation, shop-floor access to drawings/models and AR-assisted inspection workflows.

In the NEXUS architecture, FabStation would not be copied or replaced. It would sit as a specialist AR/fabrication module connected to live project context: what is required, where it goes, what has changed, what is installed and what is signed off.
