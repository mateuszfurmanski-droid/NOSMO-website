Why this matters

DoorFlow starts with doors because doors are document-heavy, schedule-heavy and inspection-heavy. The same logic can extend into fabrication-heavy trades where workshop data, site status and QA evidence need to stay connected.

NEXUS can act as the memory and routing layer between office, workshop and site. A specialist AR module can then receive cleaner packages instead of disconnected files, screenshots and outdated instructions.
